🔑 Key Findings

Four critical insights from 113 survey responses

🏆

80% Ready

Average readiness score of 12.75 out of 16 — Bhutan shows strong foundational readiness for cryptocurrency.

👂

High Awareness, Low Understanding

96% have heard of crypto but most only have basic understanding — a critical knowledge gap exists.

🌐

Internet is Barrier #1

Poor internet connection is the single biggest barrier — especially critical for rural communities.

📅

Age Over Location

Age predicts readiness more strongly than location — younger groups show consistently higher scores.
